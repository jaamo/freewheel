## Random

- bpm 40
- 1 beat is 1.5 s
- 1 bar is four beats and total of 6 seconds
- 4 bars is a section duration of 24 seconds

Atmospheres

- 1 section fade in + 4 sections constant volume + 1 section fade out
- 24s + 96s + 24s
